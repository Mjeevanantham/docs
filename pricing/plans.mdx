## Plan Limits

### Agent Limits

- **Free**: 1 agent
- **Starter**: 3 agents
- **Business**: 10 agents
- **Pro**: Unlimited agents
- **Enterprise**: Unlimited agents

### Message Limits

Message limits reset monthly. Unused messages don't roll over.

- **Free**: 100 messages/month
- **Starter**: 5,000 messages/month
- **Business**: 25,000 messages/month
- **Pro**: 100,000 messages/month
- **Enterprise**: Custom limits

### Knowledge Base Limits

- **Free**: 10 documents, 5MB total
- **Starter**: 100 documents, 100MB total
- **Business**: Unlimited documents, 1GB total
- **Pro**: Unlimited documents, 10GB total
- **Enterprise**: Unlimited documents, custom storage

### URL Scraping Limits

- **Free**: Not available
- **Starter**: 100 pages/month
- **Business**: 1,000 pages/month
- **Pro**: 10,000 pages/month
- **Enterprise**: Custom limits

## Billing

### Payment Methods

- Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- Debit card
- PayPal (select plans)
- Bank transfer (Enterprise)

### Billing Cycle

- **Monthly**: Billed monthly
- **Yearly**: Billed annually (save 17%)

### Upgrades and Downgrades

- **Upgrade**: Immediate, prorated billing
- **Downgrade**: Takes effect at next billing cycle
- **Cancellation**: Access continues until end of billing period

## Usage Monitoring

### Viewing Usage

1. Go to your dashboard
2. Navigate to **Usage** or **Billing**
3. View current usage:
   - Messages used/limit
   - Agents created/limit
   - Knowledge base size
   - URL scraping pages used/limit

### Usage Alerts

- **80% Warning**: Email alert at 80% of limit
- **100% Limit**: Service paused at limit (can upgrade)
- **Daily Updates**: Daily usage summary emails

## FAQ

### Can I change plans anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, downgrades at the next billing cycle.

### What happens if I exceed limits?

You'll receive warnings at 80% and 100% of your limits. At 100%, service may be paused until you upgrade or the limit resets.

### Do unused messages roll over?

No, message limits reset monthly and unused messages don't roll over.

### Can I get a refund?

Monthly plans: No refunds, cancel anytime. Yearly plans: Prorated refund within 30 days.

### Is there a free trial?

The Free plan is available forever with no credit card required. Paid plans may offer free trials - check current promotions.
